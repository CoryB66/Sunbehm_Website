A Special Service

Recovery Watch

When a homeowner is in the hospital, a rehab facility, or a transitional care unit, their home shouldn't be left to fend for itself. Recovery Watch keeps a careful eye on the property throughout the stay — handling mail, checking on things, and making sure home is safe and ready for the day they return.

For families who can't be there in person, it's the reassurance that someone trustworthy and local is looking after what matters.

Watch begins within 48 hours of authorization.
