A local neighbor watching over your home.

Sunbehm Home Watch & Concierge is locally owned and rooted right here in lakes country. When you're away for the season, your cabin, lake home, or condo deserves more than a glance through a window — it deserves a careful, trained eye and a familiar face.

Every visit is thorough, documented, and reported back to you with photos. If something needs attention, you'll know right away — and we'll help coordinate whatever comes next.

This is a community we're invested in. Your peace of mind is the whole point.

What We Do

Services

Comprehensive care for seasonal and lake properties — from routine watch visits to the extra help that makes coming home effortless.

01

Home Watch

Regular interior and exterior inspections on a weekly or biweekly schedule. We check for leaks, intrusion, mechanical issues, pests, and weather damage — then send you a photo report after every visit.

02

Concierge

The extra hands you need from afar — mail and package handling, contractor access, arrival and departure prep, vehicle starting, and the small tasks that keep your property ready and welcoming.

03

Snow Removal

Reliable winter clearing so your driveway and walkways stay safe and accessible all season. Coordinated per snowfall, with clear per-event pricing and no surprises.
